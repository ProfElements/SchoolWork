The Rules of Multiplication

  1. Same signs - the product is positive
  2. Opposite signs - the product is negative
  3. The product is an even number of negatives is positive
  4. The product of an odd number of negatives is negative

When using the distributive property, be sure to multiply both of the numbers inside the parentheses by the number outside the parentheses. Look at these examples:

Examples of Multiplication Rules

  • +2 x +3 = +6
  • -2 x +3 = -6
  • -2 x -3 = +6
